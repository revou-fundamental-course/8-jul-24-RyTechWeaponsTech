BMI Calculator

This calculator's purpose is to calculate your BMI. It is built for everyone, regardless of age and gender. Please be mindful that it is recommended to consult to the professionals if you find yourself in any abnormal category after calculation.
The calculator will give you an insight of the comparison between your weight and its general value. The BMI will be calculated using your weight (kg) divided by the quadratic of your height (meter) [BMI = Kg/M^2]
